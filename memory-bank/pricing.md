# Pricing — Beacon (source of truth)

Beacon is billed per user, per month.

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 1 project, up to 3 users |
| Pro  | **$29 / user / month** | unlimited projects, integrations, changelog export |
| Team | $49 / user / month | SSO, audit log, priority support |

## Billing logic
- Seats are counted at the start of each billing cycle.
- Annual plans get 2 months free (pay for 10).
- Upgrades are prorated immediately; downgrades take effect at the next cycle.
